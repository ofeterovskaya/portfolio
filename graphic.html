Web Graphic Experience

I used pen tool to outline the objects and using gradient tool and different brushes draw the the objects. Also I used eye drop tool to pick up the same colors as at the photo. But the most helpfull tool for me was mesh tool, which helps me to add more gradient colors for background and for flower to make them more realistic.

I used the Adobe Illustrator creating this icon. I used some effects such as: bevel effect, distort and transform effects of Illustrator. This icon can be used by dealerships for their web sites as a 3D icon, or 3D clickable button as well.

The main idea is the split personality, the girs' double life. Angel during the day and living in the village, and the Devil during the night time in the big city. I created sketch of the devil face to use it as a mask for the fire doing the blending effect. Also I used different kind of brushes to make fire more realistic, unnecessary parts of fire I deleted using masking effect. Also I played with color balance, hue, and contrast to make all elements fit together.
